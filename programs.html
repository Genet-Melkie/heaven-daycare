Infants Program

Our infant program provides a nurturing, safe environment where your baby can develop at their own pace. We focus on creating strong bonds between caregivers and infants to build trust and security.

Program Highlights:

  • Low teacher-to-child ratio (1:3) for personalized care
  • Daily communication with parents through our app
  • Sensory-rich environment to stimulate development
  • Customized care routines following parents' preferences
  • Tummy time and physical development activities

Toddlers Program

Our toddler program embraces children's natural curiosity and growing independence. We provide opportunities for exploration, social interaction, and emerging language development in a safe, stimulating environment.

Program Highlights:

  • Teacher-to-child ratio of 1:5 for attentive supervision
  • Introduction to routines and group activities
  • Focus on language acquisition and communication skills
  • Toilet training support when your child is ready
  • Fine and gross motor skill development

Preschool Program

Our preschool program prepares children for success in kindergarten and beyond. Through a balance of structured learning and creative play, children develop social skills, early literacy, mathematical concepts, and scientific thinking.

Program Highlights:

  • Teacher-to-child ratio of 1:8 allowing individual attention
  • Comprehensive school readiness curriculum
  • Focus on early literacy and pre-reading skills
  • Introduction to mathematical concepts through hands-on activities
  • Social-emotional development and conflict resolution skills

After School Program

Our after-school program provides a safe, enriching environment for school-age children. We balance homework support with recreational activities, giving children time to learn, play, and socialize after a day at school.

Program Highlights:

  • Transportation from local schools to our facility
  • Dedicated homework time with staff assistance
  • STEM activities, arts and crafts, and physical games
  • Special interest clubs and projects
  • Full-day care during school holidays and breaks
