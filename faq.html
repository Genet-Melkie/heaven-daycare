Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions!

Heaven Daycare is open Monday through Friday from 6:30 AM to 6:30 PM. We are closed on weekends, major holidays, and for staff development days (which are announced at least one month in advance).

The enrollment process includes these steps:

  1. Schedule a tour to visit our facility
  2. Complete the registration form and pay the registration fee
  3. Submit required documentation (immunization records, emergency contacts, etc.)
  4. Attend parent orientation
  5. Schedule your child's start date

We recommend starting the enrollment process at least 2-4 weeks before your desired start date.

We maintain low ratios to ensure quality care and individualized attention:

  • Infants (0-18 months): 1:3
  • Toddlers (18-36 months): 1:5
  • Preschool (3-5 years): 1:8
  • After School (5-12 years): 1:12

These ratios exceed state requirements to provide the best care possible.

Yes, we provide nutritious meals and snacks that are included in your tuition:

  • Breakfast: 8:00 AM - 8:30 AM
  • Morning Snack: 10:00 AM - 10:30 AM
  • Lunch: 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM (varies by age group)
  • Afternoon Snack: 2:30 PM - 3:00 PM

Our menu rotates on a 4-week cycle and is planned by a nutrition specialist. Weekly menus are posted in each classroom and on our parent portal.

Your child's safety is our top priority. Our security measures include:

  • Secure access system with unique family codes
  • Video monitoring throughout the facility
  • Strict check-in/check-out procedures
  • Authorized pick-up lists for each child
  • Regular emergency drills
  • Background checks for all staff
