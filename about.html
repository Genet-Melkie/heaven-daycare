About Heaven Daycare

Learn about our mission, values, and the dedicated team behind our quality childcare

Our Story

Heaven Daycare was founded in 2010 by Emily Johnson, a former elementary school teacher with a passion for early childhood education. After becoming a mother herself, Emily recognized the need for high-quality childcare that truly nurtures the whole child.

What began as a small home-based daycare with just six children has grown into a respected childcare center serving over 100 families in our community. Throughout our growth, we've maintained our founding principles of providing loving care in a stimulating environment where children can develop to their fullest potential.

Today, Heaven Daycare is recognized as a leader in early childhood education, with a waiting list of families eager to join our community. We continue to evolve our programs based on the latest research while staying true to our core belief that children learn best through play, exploration, and meaningful relationships.

Our team of dedicated educators shares a common goal: to create a foundation for lifelong learning by fostering curiosity, confidence, and compassion in every child who walks through our doors.

Our Mission & Values

The guiding principles that shape everything we do

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing, educational environment where children can develop socially, emotionally, physically, and intellectually at their own pace, supported by caring educators who recognize and celebrate each child's unique qualities and potential.

Nurturing Relationships

We believe secure attachments are the foundation for healthy development and learning. Our educators build warm, responsive relationships with each child and family.

Child-Centered Approach

We respect children as capable learners and design our environment and curriculum to follow their interests while supporting their development.

Community & Inclusion

We celebrate diversity and create an inclusive environment where all children and families feel welcomed, respected, and valued as part of our community.

Continuous Learning

We stay informed about the latest research in child development and early education, continuously improving our practices to benefit the children we serve.

Family Partnership

We view parents as the child's first and most important teachers, and we work together to support each child's growth and development.

Environmental Stewardship

We foster a connection to nature and teach children to respect and care for their environment through sustainable practices and outdoor learning.
